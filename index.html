my story

Due to my long-standing enthusiasm for fashion, my previous education and my current studies, I have a high degree of creativity and discipline. I am team-oriented and highly reliable. After my apprenticeship, I decided to study economy, as I firmly believe that business operations are an additional important aspect of fashion. This combination enables me to solve problems from two perspectives: the designer's and the economist's view.

take your business directly to success

For me, becoming a fashion designer was an amazing sense of achievement and joy has I had the opportunity to see my own fashion line becoming a reality on the catwalk. What fascinates me the most about fashion industry is the ability to permanently identify new trends and then make them available to customers in a targeted and timely manner. I already gained practical experience in a 6-week internship at the s.Oliver HQ in Rottendorf. As an intern in the buying and e-commerce unit, it was my task to indentify new trends for the spring / summer 2017 season with the WGSN portal. Additionally, I supported the order phase and the general operative business.
